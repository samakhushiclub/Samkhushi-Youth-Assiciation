About Us

Samakhushi Youth Association is a non-profit social club established nearly five decades ago and legally registered for over three decades. The club is steered by a group of skilled and matured community members. Committee members are elected by the General Assembly for a four-year term and volunteer their time with full transparency. The annual assembly approves audit reports and reviews completed social initiatives.

Our Mission

To promote social well-being, youth empowerment, community development, and transparent governance through volunteer-driven initiatives and programs.
